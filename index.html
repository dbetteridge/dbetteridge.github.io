Services

IT support

Day-to-day technology support for businesses that need reliable help without signing up to a large managed service contract.

  • Fixing computers, networks, and connectivity issues
  • Setting up new devices, accounts, and software
  • Troubleshooting email, printing, and shared tools
  • Keeping systems secure and sensibly maintained
  • Explaining options in plain language so you can decide what is worth doing

Startup consulting

Technology guidance for early-stage founders — helping you choose the right approach, avoid costly mistakes, and build on foundations that will actually scale. That includes using AI tooling responsibly and catching problems before they reach production.

  • Architecture advice — monoliths, microservices, and when each makes sense
  • Web and API development with React, JavaScript, Node, and GraphQL
  • Cloud and infrastructure on AWS
  • Greenfield builds and improving existing (brownfield) systems
  • Security — auth, data handling, and deployment practices suited to your stage
  • AI-assisted development — using Claude Code, AWS Bedrock, and Cursor safely, and reviewing generated code for security holes, bugs, and shortcuts before they ship

About

I'm Daniel Betteridge, based in Western Australia's South West. I work with businesses across the region, with a particular focus on the Cape to Cape area.

I spent five years in SME IT support and customer service before moving into software development. Over the past twelve years I've worked full stack across small startups and larger multinational businesses — on both greenfield projects and brownfield systems that needed careful improvement.
